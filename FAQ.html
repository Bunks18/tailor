What is your refund policy?

We offer a full return policy if you are not satisifed with your built out itinerary. If you wish to seek a reimbursement for the money spent on your itinerary simply email us at tailoredtravelsinfo@gmail.com, and we will be happy to refund you your money.

Do you book flights and hotels?

We do not perform booking services. Your itinerary will provide you with a list of suggested places to visit and lodging however you are responsible for handling all booking for your future travel plans.

Do you lead trips?

No. We are a travel planning services company. If you are interested in guided tours in particular locations we can provide you with a list of contacts who we have used in the past.
